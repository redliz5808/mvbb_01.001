Last updated: 04/20/2026

This Sales & Refund Policy (“Policy”) governs all purchases made through Mahoning Valley Book Bazaar (“MVBB,” “we,” “our,” or “us”), including event tickets, vendor table fees, and MVBB-operated merchandise.

By completing a purchase with MVBB, you agree to the terms outlined in this Policy.

MVBB reserves the right to refuse or cancel any order at its discretion.

Sales Terms

All sales are final.

Customers should contact vendors directly regarding any issues.

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting MVBB may result in:

We encourage all customers and vendors to contact us directly to resolve any issues.
